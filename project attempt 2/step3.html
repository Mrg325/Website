Step 3

This part you will need to catch between 30 petal shells, that you will turn in for Fisher's Gobbiegoo

This part of the questline is slightly different. You will be catching Petal Shells in Yanxia, on a small island towards the west side of the area. This time after catching all 30, you will turn them in to the merchant next to the person who gave you the quest for an item called Fisher's Gobbiegoo. Then you will be able to complete the quest. When catching the Petal Shell's patience is not REQUIRED but it is still recommended to use as it will make the process much faster.

This is the item that you will turn the Petal Shell's in for. The merchants name is Deny's and he is inside the building next to where you recieve the quest.

This is the item you will be fishing for!

Here is a more percise view of the spot in Yanxia where you will be fishing. It is a lone island off to the right.
