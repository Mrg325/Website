Step 5

Here you the amount of fish you catch will vary as a new mechanic called "collectables is involved

You're finally at the last step! This part will take the longest as you now have to deal with collectables. This means that when you catch the fish, it will have a collectability rating, and the higher that rating is, the more items you get when you turn it in. You will need 400 "Oddly delicate fishing reel parts" The highest one catch can give you is 4, so you will be here a while! You will be going back to Azys La, but instead towards the southwest side of the area, on an island. Once again you NEED to use Patience along with powerful set when theres two exclamation points or more, and when theres one exclamation point. This is to catch the fish at the highest collectability you can. Also make sure to turn on the ability "collect" or the fish you catch won't have any collectability rating and will be worthless.

This was the final step of the FFXIV fishing relic, after this you will have your shiny fishing pole!

You will be catching pickled pom and flintstrike as shown above.

A more accurate look at island on Azys la you will be fishing at. Get comfortable you will be here for a long time!

Congradulations! After all that fishing you have finally gotten your shiny fishing pole!
