Step 1

This part of the questline will have you catching 40 Thinker's Coral

For your first quest you'll need to go to Dravanian Hinterlands. There is no direct teleport there so you must first go to idyllshire and walk from there. Once there head towards the bridge in the center of the area and Fish off the side. You will need to use "Patience" and percision hookset when seeing one excalamation point above your head. It's not worth using otherwise.

Here you are catching Thinker's Coral, shown above.

This shows more specifically on the map in Dravanian Hinterlands where to go. It is nearby the bridge close to the center.
