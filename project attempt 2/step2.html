Step 2

This part of the questline will have you catching 60 HQ Dragonspine

Head over to Coerthas Western Highlands and go towards the North West of the zone. You will find a small pond with a small snowy hill in the center. This is where you will catch the Dragonspine. You will need to use the ability "Patience" to catch them in HQ, which they have to be for the quest. You will then use Powerful Hookset whenever you see two exclamation points above your head, any other amount is not worth it. Since this will use a lot of GP it is recommended to bring Hi-cordials to make the process faster.
